International Housewares Association

Lifetime experience.

The opportunity.

I attended a program called Seed Talent Program when I was in my sophomore years, and it was a big blast. I was the captain of Mei-Fu team. Mei-Fu is a construction company, and a member of the ADA, which is a party gathered with construction companies CEOs, dedicatd to give back to the society. ADA is also a member ogf the International Housewares Association, its importance is like the WHO in construction field where countries worldwide share their new techniques and problem semi-annualy.

The Vice President?

Because this was a huge event for Taiwan being able to attend an international organization as an country, we had the honor to invite the Vice President to give the opening speech.

The IHA Chairwoman.

I was chosen as the receptionist of the IHA Chairwoman, Christine. She is a lovely lady from Australlia, we exchanged gifts from our country, and I tooke her to CKS Memorial Hall to see the Changing of the Guard.
