ASUS Artificial Intelligence Service Center

Program Management Internship.

The opportunity.

The moment I found that AICS was hiring, I thought this was my chance. As a business student it is not easy to apply for a position in a tech company given your background normally is useless to them. But this time AICS was recruiting a program management intern, and I jumped on the wagon. I was lucky enough to get the position, hence started my way on learning what is Artificial Intelligence.

AI, the future or the present?

This internship gave me the opportunity to understand AI and its application. After some thorough research, I gave myself the answer to the question, I believe AI is th present. Traditionally we will think of AI as AlphaGo, Deep Blue, those who try to win over chess or answer riddles. We are talking about those in the labs, that maybe replacing human being in a short amount of time, but those stages have passed. Now everyone can build up their own AI, maybe low level but hey it works, what people are discussing is how are we going to use it. We have passed the stage where we wonder what is AI, but moved on to what can AI do. It is clearly that AI is the present, AI applications are the future.

Back to AICS, what am I doing.

AICS is divided into four domains: smart retail, smart transportation, smart security and smart medical. I was first assigned to the smart transportation team, our goal was to produce the first MIT autonamous vehicle, this was a huge goal and the team worked very hard to produce the Advance Driver Assistance System that autonamous vehicle needed, and had a huge success. It is my pleasure to spend my days in the lab working with engineers with a goal, everyday we tried to take another step forward. Successful or not, we had an amazing time together.
