NTUFIN Gathering

Means more than it tastes.

Annual

The NTU Fin Gathering is hosted every year by the Activity Department of the Student Association of the Department of Finance. I am the minister this year therfore it was my resposibility to host it. Our thoought was to make it a barbeque party, students from different grades gather around to chat, the scene would be beautiful.

Gathering

We were able to get 200 people to attend this event, that is like 2/3 of the Department. My team and I were very proud, but we always remembered the meaning of this event, gathering. Entering your jinior year Finance students are busying working on their projects, seraching for internships or other side projects, you rarely see uour classmates. We hope to give everyone a reason, to clear their schedule for this one day, to speak with your classmates face to face.

FIN

We always call the gathering "The mysterious night". No matter what happened in the past, you can always sit down, grab a bottle of beer, and chat with strangers, enemies or your ex. Witnessing this moment every year, makes everyone willing to come here again next year. Chewing on roasted bacon, sipping beer, listening what your friends are up to, this is a magical night.
