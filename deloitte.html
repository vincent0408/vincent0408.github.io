Deloitte Taiwan

Audit Internship.

Work at Deloitte.

November of 2019, I made a decision to apply for an audit internship at Deloitte. I knew the busy season was about to start, and I was aiming for it. I wanted to know what it would be like working at Deloitte, the world's big4 accounting firm, especially in the busy season as an audit intern.
I was thrilled when I pass the interview and got this opportunity to be an employee at Deloitte. My job was to access clients' journal entries and trial balance sheet using Microsoft SQL Server. I would analyze the data by several standards to check if the data is appropriate without any mistakes, then I would transfer it to our audit team for thorough research of errors.

Importance of the internship.

This internship was important to me because I got a chance to use my coding skills while in a more business related company. I have always believed business and computer science together would be our future, but that was just my thoughts. I got the confirmation though, at Deloitte, somewhere I would be least expecting my coding skills to be in handy.

Technology leading imporovement.

Deloitte started to speed up the audit process several years ago, and there is already a not perfect, but pretty good audit system to replace the repetitive parts of auditing. Now you can get the same amount of information from SQL compared to the old flipping through subpoena, but in 100 times faster. You can calculate the sum of anything you want, rather than using the calculator while worrying if you made a typo. Even accounting firms are using code to not only speed up but also more productive on their work, not to mention other companies which have more ways of implementing automate systems in their working environment.
